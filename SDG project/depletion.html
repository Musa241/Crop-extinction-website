Groundwater Depletion and Water Quality Issues

Groundwater is over-extracted in some parts of the city, and depletion is already felt, particularly in peri-urban areas that do not have piped water infrastructures. In Hyderabad, there are also problems with water quality. Fluoride, nitrates, and heavy metals have been found in a few regions and even in groundwater. Because of the poor practices in the treatment of water in some localities, water-borne diseases are present.

Sanitation Challenges in Hyderabad

Despite achieving significant improvement in sanitation conditions in Hyderabad, quality sanitation access is still uneven and lacking everywhere in most informal settlements and peri-urban zones. Most households and communities still rely on pit latrines or open defecation due to lack of access to proper sewerage systems.

Inadequate Sanitation

Limited access to proper sanitation facilities in informal settlements and peri-urban areas.

Lack of Sewerage Systems

Many households rely on pit latrines or open defecation due to the absence of proper sewerage systems.

Health Risks

Inadequate sanitation poses significant health risks to communities.
