Addressing the Global Water and Sanitation Crisis

The lack of access to clean water and sanitation is a critical global problem affecting hundreds of millions of people. Approximately 2 billion people lack access to safe drinking water, and over 3.6 billion use sanitation services that leave human waste untreated. This lack of access has devastating consequences, including preventable deaths, particularly among children. At least 1.4 million people die each year from preventable causes linked to unsafe water and poor sanitation.
