Addressing the Problem: A Multi-faceted Approach

To address the problem of clean water and sanitation, action and coordination among governments, organizations, and people are required. Governments must begin by increasing political commitment for safely managed drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene. Effective service delivery would require proper governance structures, enforcement of health-based standards, and an application of health-based standards, with transparency in monitoring and reporting progress.

1

Increased Political Commitment

Governments must prioritize clean water and sanitation initiatives.

2

Effective Service Delivery

Proper governance structures, enforcement of health-based standards, and transparent monitoring are crucial.

3

Community Engagement

Involving communities in planning and implementation is essential for sustainable solutions.
