The Importance of Addressing the Problem

The social and economic impacts of inadequate access to water and sanitation are significant. A lack of proper access leaves people ill, girls out of school, and entire communities displaced by a scarcity of water. Additionally, the economic cost of poor sanitation is severe. Annually, worldwide, it is estimated that a lack of proper sanitation costs the world about US$223 billion.

Health Impacts

Inadequate access to clean water and sanitation leads to preventable deaths, especially among children.

Social Impacts

Lack of access to clean water and sanitation can lead to illness, school absenteeism, and displacement.

Economic Impacts

Poor sanitation costs the world an estimated US$223 billion annually.
